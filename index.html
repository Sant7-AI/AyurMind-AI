This app provides general Ayurvedic information based on classical texts (Charaka Samhita, Ashtanga Hridayam, Sushruta Samhita) and peer-reviewed research. It does not constitute medical diagnosis, clinical advice, or a substitute for professional consultation.

Before starting any herbal treatment, diet change, or Panchakarma therapy, always consult:

• Your registered medical doctor (MBBS/MD)

Prakriti assessment by questionnaire is a self-awareness tool. For clinical Prakriti determination, in-person Dashavidha Pareeksha (ten-fold examination) by a trained Vaidya is required. (Charaka Samhita Vimanasthana 8.67)
